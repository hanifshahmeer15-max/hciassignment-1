GOMS Model (Using M, K, P Only)

1. Wi-Fi ON Activity

Goal:

Turn Wi-Fi ON

Operators:
  • M – Mental preparation (thinking)
  • P – Pointing (move finger/cursor)
  • K – Keystroke or tap
Method (Operator Sequence):
M → P → K → P → K → P → K
Step-by-Step (MKP):
  1. M – Think: I need Wi-Fi.
  2. P – Point to phone screen.
  3. K – Unlock the phone.
  4. P – Point to notification panel.
  5. K – Swipe down the panel.
  6. P – Point to Wi-Fi icon.
  7. K – Tap the Wi-Fi icon.

2. Who Paid Problem

Goal:

Find out who paid the bill

Operators:
  • M – Mental preparation (thinking)
  • P – Pointing (move finger/cursor)
  • K – Keystroke or tap
Method (Operator Sequence):
M → P → K → P → K → P → K → M
Step-by-Step (MKP):
  1. M – Think: Check who paid.
  2. P – Point to payment app.
  3. K – Open the app.
  4. P – Point to transaction history.
  5. K – Open history.
  6. P – Point to specific transaction.
  7. K – Open transaction.
  8. M – Read payer name.
